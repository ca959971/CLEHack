Prizes

Three overall prizes will be awarded to three teams that have the best overall idea, the best non-technical idea, and the best technical solution.

Four additional category prizes will be given to teams that have demonstrated exceptionally creative, innovative, or thorough use of data in their particular track in their solution. Participants are eligible to win both overall prizes and category prizes.
