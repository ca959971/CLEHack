Chartered in 1933, the Cuyahoga Metropolitan Housing Authority (CMHA) is one of the oldest and largest housing authorities in America. CMHA strives to be the leader in cultivating safe, sustainable, dynamic communities of choice. For more information about CMHA, click here.

Problem Statements

How can current and prospective Housing Choice Voucher recipients be most effectively prepared to make a decision about where they choose to live? Several factors influence this selection, but four elements contribute greatly to the foundation of a well-resourced neighborhood. We invite you develop a creative solution for one or more of the following problem statements:

Transportation How do we evaluate the accessibility of well-resourced neighborhoods, in order to increase the mobility of low income households who do not have a personal vehicle?

Education How can we help families by ensuring that they have the opportunity to send their children to schools with the best educational resources to serve their individual needs?Education How can we help families by ensuring that they have the opportunity to send their children to schools with the best educational resources to serve their individual needs?

Wellness How can we help people determine that a neighborhood will provide the amenities that new members require in order to thrive, no matter their demographic, and become a long lasting asset to the community?

Connectivity How do we evaluate neighborhoods for high speed internet connectivity, and computer access, in order to ensure that households move to areas that provide them with the technological resources necessary to achieve their goals?
