CMHA and DigitalC and Cleveland State University are partnering to host the Cleveland Housing Hackathon. The hackathon will aim aid the HCVP, Housing Choice Voucher Program, in its goal of finding well-resourced to increase mobility. Employment, retail, education, transportation and healthcare are among the factors to be considered when employing solutions. The hackathon will hope to produce either an app, visualization or product to aid the HCVP using data based on four interlocking tracks: transportation, wellness, education and connectivity.
